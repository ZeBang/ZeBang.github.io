Time Granuation

Structure

Time series prediction is a huge topic. In most cases, specifically, we want to predict \( (x,t) \) where \(x\) is value at time point \(t\), i.e.: $$\mathbb{P}( x=x_0, t=t_0)$$ However, this leads to: (1) more accurate we want then more challenge we have to deal with; (2) this type of accurate is not necessary. Two major modification can be: $$ \mathbb{P}( x=x_0, t \in B) $$ or $$ \mathbb{P}( x \in A, t \in B) $$ As the set \(A, B\) itself can be a function of time \(T\), we call such data structure \( ( x \in A_T, t \in B_T) \) as time-series granuation.
